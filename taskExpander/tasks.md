- Bake a cake
  - Gather all necessary ingredients (flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, vanilla extract, etc.).
  - Preheat the oven to the required temperature (usually 350°F or 175°C).
  - Prepare the baking pan by greasing and lining it with parchment paper.
  - Mix dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, baking powder, and salt).
  - Cream butter and sugar together in a separate bowl until light and fluffy.
  - Add eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture, and mix until combined.
  - Gradually combine the dry ingredients with the wet mixture, and pour the batter into the prepared pan.
